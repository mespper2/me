About Me

I'm Messias Pereira, a post-career software developer and writer.

My interests include:

  • Software Development, with a particular focus at the moment on 3D Web / WebXR
  • Home Education and Democratic Schooling
  • Nonviolent Communication and Self-Determination Theory
  • Software Quality and Reliability

Previously, I spent a 20 year career working at Metaswitch Networks (now Microsoft Azure for Operators) in a wide range of technical and leadership roles.

A lot of the work I do these days is pro bono and/or self-directed and/or open-source.

I do also take on some paid contract work and consultancy. If you have a project that you think I could help with, please do reach out.
